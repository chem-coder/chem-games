## Product Frame

Chem Games is a Duolingo-style chemistry learning app with fun games that help students master the basics of general chemistry.

Each topic may be accompanied by YouTube videos explaining the concept and linking back to the relevant app or practice tool.

## Scientific Method

- Practice the standard scientific method steps.
- Use real or AI-generated examples for repeated practice.

- 1 ft = 12 in
- 1 yd = 3 ft

- 1 lb = 453.592 g
- 1 kg = 2.20462 lb
- 1 lb = 16 oz
- 1 min = 60 s
- 1 hr = 60 min
- 1 day = 24 hr
- 1 year = 365 days
- Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin temperature conversions
- 1 gallon = 4 qt
- 1 quart = 32 fl oz
- 1 pint = 16 fl oz
- 1 cup = 8 fl oz
- 1 quart = 2 pints = 4 cups = 32 fl oz
- 1 L = 1.05669 qt, approximately

## Nomenclature Quiz Generator

Formula-to-name mode:

- Generate a random compound.
- Show a generated formula or formula pattern, such as `MxXOy`.
- Ask the student to enter the compound name.
- Include a hint button that reveals the ion names.
- Include an answer button that shows the correct compound name.

Name-to-formula mode:

- Generate a random compound name.
- Ask the student to enter the chemical formula.
- Include a hint button that reveals the ions and names.
- Include an answer button that shows the correct chemical formula.
